About AdTechmedia

AdTechMedia is on a mission to build the world's most simple content monetization product. Imagine you are a publisher, a journalist or a blogger, and your core business is to create content that people love. Do you have extra time in your busy schedule to learn how to setup and manage Apple Pay or PayPal, Google Doubleclick For Publishers or Revive Ad Server, JavaScript or NodeJS? If the answer is strong NO, please continue reading...

AdTechMedia product simplifies content creators journey to revenue generation. In just couple of clicks, you can:

  • Install and activate WordPress plugin from wordpress.org (or Drupal module from drupal.org), no technical skills required;
  • Choose the revenue options your readers will be seamlessly provided;
  • Customize any other look and feel settings that will make AdTechMedia plugin perform natively on your website.

The Challenge

The success of our business is directly dependent on how fast and how easy content consumers are able to go through monetization process in order to consume content. We provide native look and feel, meaning AdTechMedia's code is pre-loaded on customers' websites and executed in most optimal way as any other first party script. Any unnecessary steps or unwanted delays in monetization workflow will result in lost revenue, and nobody wants that.

Why Mitoc Group?

We have been working with serverless computing for some time and we absolutely love it. The promise of managed scalability and the breath of cloud native services that proved sustainability over time made us to choose Amazon Web Services. Mitoc Group, an AWS Advanced Technology Partner and featured AWS Lambda Partner, is one of pioneers and early adopters of serverless architecture. Team's skills and experience cultivated trust and confidence that AdTechMedia engineering effort is in the right hands.

The Benefits

AWS Serverless provides an incredibly rich suite of next generation cloud native services. Mitoc Group made it easier and quicker to build and release software, as well as manage services and associated resources. We increased development agility and reduced product's time to market. Engaging continuous integration and continuous deployment processes allowed us to remain a small team with a tiny budget.
