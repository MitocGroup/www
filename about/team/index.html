Leadership Team

Eugene Istrati

Eugene Istrati has spent almost two decades as a technologist in a variety of industries, including digital media, cloud computing, and reputation marketing. He is a visionary and goal-oriented technology leader, with demonstrated experience in planning, developing and implementing cutting edge information solutions to address business opportunities.

Before co-founding Mitoc Group, Eugene worked as Director of Engineering at Hearst Corporation. He helped iconic publisher to redefine and transform their business in the digital world. Prior, Mr. Istrati worked as Program Manager at Amazon Web Services, helping businesses and developers accelerate their cloud computing journeys on AWS cloud.

Eugene blogs occasionally and speaks at industry conferences, technology meetups and other similar events. He has presented at ServerlessConf 2017 and AWS re:Invent 2017 on his favorite topic – serverless computing.

He graduated from University of Oradea, Romania with a dual Bachelor of Science degree in Mathematics and Computer Science and a Master of Science degree in Computer Science and Engineering. His masters degree was later assessed and validated by University of Columbia, New York.

Advisory Team

Chris Resch

As EVP of Cloud Solutions Sales, Chris leads Sales, Solution Architecture, Delivery, and Specialty Practices, ensuring that 2nd Watch clients receive maximum value from their cloud investment. Most recently, Chris was Practice Manager at Amazon Web Services, where he helped build the NY Metro practice and led the Global Media practice for Professional Services.

During his tenure at Amazon Web Services, Chris received several awards and accolades, including “AWS Professional Services Person of the Year.” Chris began his career at Hearst Corporation, where he held various leadership positions at Hearst Magazines and Hearst Business Media.
